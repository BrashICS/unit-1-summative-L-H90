James Gosling History

Main Page Signifigance Sources

James Gosling is very well known as being the main founder of the Javascript programming language. He was also elected as a member of the national academy of engineering in the year 2004.

James Gosling was born in 1955 on May 19th in Calgary, Canada. During the start of his academics, he went to William Aberhart High School and then Graduated to go study at University of Calgary, where he earned his Bachelor of Science degree. He also received his M.A. and Ph.D from Carnegie Mellon University, each in computer science.


Quick Facts

  • James Gosling was born on May 19, 1955, in Calgary, Alberta, Canada.
  • He earned a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from the University of Calgary in 1977.
  • Gosling completed his Ph.D. at Carnegie Mellon University in 1983, where he developed the “Gosling Emacs” text editor.
  • He is best known as the creator of the Java programming language, which he developed at Sun Microsystems in the early 1990s.
  • In 1995, Java was officially launched, and it became one of the most popular programming languages globally.
  • Gosling is also known for his contributions to computer science, including work on the NeWS windowing system.
  • After Sun Microsystems was acquired by Oracle Corporation in 2010, he became a key advocate for open source software.
  • Throughout his career, Gosling has received numerous awards and honors for his work in technology and programming.
